About Us

Shuei-Do Manju Shop has been family owned and operated for over 68 years. We are currently the only traditional Japanese confectionery in our county. Our goal is to share our love for sweets and traditions with our community. You can find our shop located in the heart of Japantown in San Jose, California.

What We Stand For

Here at Shuei-Do Manju Shop, we believe in always providing our customers with the highest quality ingredients. To ensure that we deliver the highest quality, we make everything in-house and homemade daily without the use of preservatives or additives.
