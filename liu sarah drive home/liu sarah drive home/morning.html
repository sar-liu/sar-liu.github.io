Drive Home

It's probably best to stay here until morning. The second you collapse onto the bed, you fall asleep, giving your aching bones and tired mind a well-earned rest.

"U-um... hello? Uh... you're kinda sleeping in my bed...? Which is okay but, I mean, it's kinda weird seeing as-"
You startle awake, jumping to your feet.
"-Whoa! It's okay, I'm not gonna hurt you! I- I'm Eden."

It's bright outside again, and someone is standing in front of you with a worried expression. They must live here.

"Uh. Amara. Sorry I broke into your house, I just… last night I crashed and my car is at the bottom of the river and then I thought I saw a-"
"Whoa whoa whoa, wait, what?! Are you okay?! How long were you out there? Do you need a ride back into town?"
You breath out a sigh of relief, calming down for the first time in a while.
“Yeah, that would be perfect.”
