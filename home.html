Shuei-Do Manju Shop is Santa Clara county's only traditional Japanese confectionery.

All our manju is handmade fresh daily, without any preservatives.

What is manju?

Manju is a Japanese confection with a soft outside made from wheat or rice flour and is traditionally filled with a sweet bean paste.

Our manju range from classic flavors, such as red bean or lima bean filling, to more contemporary flavors, including peanut butter manju and fruit flavored mochi.
