Drive Home

After a few hours' drive, you step out of their car, back at your apartment.

"Thanks again, I don't know what would have happened if you hadn't found me."
"No problem… but Amara? Can I ask you for a favor?
"Yeah, what is it?"
They unclip the colored lenses from their glasses, fiddling with it in their hands.

"Can you keep what you saw last night between us, please?"
"Huh? What do you mean-"
They look at you, and you see their eyes for the first time. Bright yellow. Glowing.

At that moment, Rei bursts out the door and throws her arms around you, knocking you off balance.

"Oh thank God! I was so scared you were… That's it, you can never leave again!"
When you glance back at the road, Eden is nowhere to be seen.
