Drive Home

"Hello? Sorry to bother you, but I really need... help..."
As you turn the knob, the door opens, unlocked. You tentatively walk inside and call out a few more times, but no one's there. You must have been imagining things.

That is, until you hear loud footsteps behind you and heavy breathing that sounded more animal like than human.

"No way... it can't be..."
